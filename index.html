Views from the Hotel

Shaanti aur khubsurti ka perfect combo yahin milega

Hamare hotel se har taraf sirf pahaadon ki khoobsurti hi nazar aati hai. Subah uthte hi aapko clouds ke beech se nikli dhoop aur door tak faile hue green valleys ka nazara milega – bilkul postcard jaise!

Chahe aap apne room ki window se dekhein ya balcony mein chai ke saath baith kar enjoy karein, yeh views aapka din bana denge. Har season ka apna hi charm hai – barf se dhake pahaad, ya monsoon ki greenery.

Agar aap nature lover hain ya bas kuch time shaanti se bitana chahte hain, toh yeh jagah aapke liye perfect hai. Yahaan aake dil aur dimaag dono fresh ho jaate hain.

Nearby Attractions

Explore the wonders around us

Kedarnath

Located just a 15-minute drive from our hotel, Gaurikund is the starting point for the Kedarnath trek. From there, embark on a scenic 14 km hike through the stunning Himalayan landscape. The trek typically takes around 6–8 hours, offering breathtaking views and an unforgettable spiritual journey.

14 km trek from Gaurikund 6-8 hours

Tungnath Temple

The trek to the revered Tungnath Temple starts from Chopta. The 4 km hike takes approximately 3-4 hours, offering stunning views of the Himalayan peaks and the surrounding beauty.

4 km trek 3-4 hours

Kalimath Temple

A sacred and serene temple, located about an hour’s drive from the hotel. This peaceful spot is known for its spiritual significance and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

1 hour drive 2-4 hours
